# Brand Intelligence

## Candidate context
This is an independent candidate campaign for a confidential Data & AI Enablement Lead opportunity represented by CFS. CFS is the recruiting firm, not asserted to be the undisclosed end employer. The campaign uses CFS identity only for nominative identification of the recruiter and opportunity source.

## Color token provenance
- `#30246c` - source-sampled from the official CFS logo; employer recognition layer.
- `#21184e` - source-sampled/contrast-adjusted deep purple used for accessible large surfaces.
- `#ffffff`, `#17151d` - source-sampled neutral/ink behavior.
- `#e05a47` - candidate-original focus accent representing intervention and attention.
- `#14877e` - candidate-original signal accent representing trusted evidence.
- Light tints are candidate-derived accessible surface colors and are not represented as official CFS colors.

## Typography evidence and decision
The current CFS site presents a clean, modern sans-serif system with bold, compact headings and readable body copy. No public brand guide or authoritative public font name was located during research. The campaign uses licensed Google-hosted Lato when available and a documented system sans fallback. This is a structural substitute, not a claim that Lato is CFS's official typeface. No proprietary font files are copied or committed.

## Photography, geometry, and motion
CFS public pages use people-first recruiting photography, generous white space, purple recognition cues, rounded calls to action, and a direct relationship-oriented tone. Because the client is confidential and the campaign argument is technical/operating, the campaign does not use stock people photography. Employer recognition comes from the official mark, sampled palette, clean typographic hierarchy, white space, and restrained rounded controls.

The candidate-original role system is a Shared Operating Lens: fragmented source signals converge through an aperture into a focused operating state. Motion shows source drift, lens focus, and scenario-driven state changes. It is separate from and does not alter the CFS logo.

## Recommended use by artifact
- Website: fullest official CFS identity plus Shared Operating Lens.
- Resume and cover letter: restrained CFS mark, purple rules, and independent-candidate qualifier.
- Interview brief, 120-day plan, and review canvas: same document system, role accent, and neutral candidate context.
- PDFs: preserve visible contact information, official mark, exact pagination, and independent-candidate distinction.
